Neige

I'm the human of a wonderful cat called Neige.

We met in 2011 at Peninsula Humane Society & SPCA. She was a beautiful white kitten with a super-soft fur. It was Love at first sight, she moved in immediatly after our first date.

Neige is my first cat ever (I grew up with dogs). One of my friends who has kids says "I didn't know how to be a parent. It's my kid who taught me how to be a parent." I guess it's the same thing with cats. Neige taught me how to be a good cat dad.
