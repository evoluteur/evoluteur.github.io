Madeleines

Ingredients for 16 madeleines:

  • 120 grams of butter
  • 120 grams of flour
  • 120 grams of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of yeast or 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • (optional) 1 lemon zest or some vanilla extract
  • (optional) pine nuts or raw sliced almonds

Tools:

  • Madeleines pan
  • Sifter

Instructions:

  1. Prepare yeast according to instructions on package
  2. Pre-heat oven at 375°F
  3. Mix soft (but not melted) butter and sugar
  4. Add eggs and flour (using the sifter) and a pinch of salt
  5. Add the yeast or baking powder
  6. (optional) Add the lemon zest or vanilla extract
  7. Mix until the dough is homogeneous
  8. Put a little melted butter on the madeleines pan (to prevent sticking)
  9. Fill each slot on the madeleines pan w/ dough
  10. (optional) Add sliced almonds or pine nuts on top
  11. Put in the oven for 10 to 15 minutes (until golden)
  12. Get the madeleines tray out of the oven, and the madelines out of the tray, and let cool down

Notes: best on the day they are baked, but can be kept for a week in a container.
